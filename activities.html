Company Expose

The Company Expose is the largest ITWS recruitment event, featureing students and recruiters engaging in casual games and activities as a way to "break the ice".

Some of these activities include Two Truths and a Lie, Pancakes vs Waffles, and Never Have I Ever.

Once the planned activities are completed, students have time to chat one-on-one with recruiters and network.

Tutoring

Gamma Nu Eta holds weekly tutoring sessions to help students with any course in their ITWS degree program.

These courses include but are not limited to ITWS core classes, such as Intro to ITWS and Web Systems, or classes outside of ITWS, such as Computer Science 1 and Data Structures.

The tutoring sessions can also be used to answer any questions about career development, resumes, or just general questions about RPI.
